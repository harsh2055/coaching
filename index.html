Required Documents

  • Recent Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Original & photocopies of previous year marksheets
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card / Passport)
  • School Leaving / Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (non-local board students)

Eligibility Criteria

SSC / State Board

Minimum 75% aggregate in qualifying Grade 10 examinations from recognized state boards.

CBSE / ICSE

Minimum 80% aggregate with Science & Mathematics as core subjects in Grade 10.

JEE / NEET Track

Students must have Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics/Biology as mandatory subjects.

Dropper Batch

Students who appeared in JEE/NEET previously are eligible. No minimum percentage required.
